# N3 Kanji Regrouping — Working Document

Status: IN PROGRESS — Wave 1 (N3.1–N3.20)

## Constraints
- N3.1 must include 忘 and 覚 (user requirement)
- Every lesson has exactly 4 kanji (except the 4 five-kanji lessons: N3.45, N3.46, N3.50, N3.80)
- Grouping priority: compound synergy > thematic coherence
- Early priorities: 友達, 昨日, 次, 一緒, 週末, 残念, 約束, game kanji (戦 守 命 危 険 殺)
